The Maid Of The Mill

This dance has been derived by the Icknield Way Morris Men.

Formation

Circular set around the fool for 6 dancers.

Style

Handkerchief.

Dance Sequence

  • Once to Yourself
  • Dance Facing
  • Distinctive Figure
  • Crossover
  • Distinctive Figure
  • Back to Back
  • Distinctive Figure
  • Whole Rounds

The Crossover is a 'belly to belly' with fool spiralling round to opposite place and then repeating the move to return.

The Back To Back is done as a standard set, 3 & 4 going left of the fool.

Note there is no Advance Meet And Retire in Maid Of The Mill

Distinctive figure

The distinctive figure is 4 x side steps facing fool and in with 2 x double steps and 2 x back steps to place. Repeat.

Handkerchiefs hang free during the clapping.

Variations

None.
