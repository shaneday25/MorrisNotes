The Black Joke

Formation

Set of 6, in 3 pairs - conventional.

Style

Stick with handkerchiefs.

Distinctive figure

The only dance to come in striking, odds hitting evens sticks at chest height, then reverse and then repeat whilst capering. This is repeated at the end of each CF. After 1st half of CO evens become odds. Odds strike evens stick at chest height thrice, on the 4th beat sticks are just pushed forward. This is repeated with evens striking followed by a Half Hey. Full repeat and Half Hey.

The Whole Rounds is only 4 double steps and 4 Plain Capers in to the centre of the set.

Variations

None.
