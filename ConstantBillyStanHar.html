Distinctive Figure

The Distinctive Figure is a clapping sequence, interpreted as 'together right, together left, together behind; together across'. This is followed by a Half Hey, a repeat of the hand clapping followed by a Half Hey to return to place.

Handkerchiefs hang free during the clapping.

Variations

According to Dommett this is a stick tapping and Half Hey dance similar for which there are two variants.

Variant A: bars 1-2 are similar to bars 1-2 of Black Joke and bars 3-4 a repeat of bars 1-2.

Variant B: bars 1-2 like bars 3-4 of Brighton Camp and bars 3-4 a repeat of bars 1-2.

Note: Trigg Morris Men use the Fieldtown 6/8 tune 'Balance the Straw' for Variant B. Hammersmith Morris Men used the Fieldtown tune ' Bobby and Joan' for the same variant but having the Dancers jump for the turns.
